DogBnB

Where your dog vacations, when you vacation.

Dog BnB was my first group project at Le Wagon. Over the course of a week, we had to come up with a web app similar to AirBnB.
No better way than to create an AirBnB experience for dogs!

My fellow group members on this project were Daniella Zimmerman, Yohann Paulin, and Bruno da Silva.

My contributions on the frontend of this page were creating the navigation bar, building the grid and cards on the Available Pup Boxes and Box Bookings pages and the view of the boxes. I also took part in some backend functionalities and database structures.

Be sure to input "Lausanne" at a 50km radius to best view all of our available Accomodations!
